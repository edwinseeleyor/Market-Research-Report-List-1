<p><strong>The Distributed Acoustic Sensing DAS System Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Longitudinal Wave Acoustic Sensing</li><li>Transverse Wave Acoustic Sensing</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Distributed Acoustic Sensing (DAS) systems utilize fiber optic cables to detect and analyze acoustic signals through two primary wave types: longitudinal and transverse. Longitudinal wave acoustic sensing measures pressure changes along the fiber, making it ideal for applications like pipeline monitoring and environmental surveying. In contrast, transverse wave acoustic sensing captures shear waves, which are particularly effective for detecting vibrations and disturbances in geophysical applications. Both market types enhance safety and surveillance in various industries by providing real-time data and insights.</p></p>
