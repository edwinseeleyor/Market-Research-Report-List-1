<p><ul><li>Trim Tabs</li><li>Interceptors</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The boat trim system market is primarily divided into two categories: trim tabs and interceptors. Trim tabs are adjustable surfaces attached to a boat's transom, enabling fine control over the vessel's angle in the water for improved stability and fuel efficiency. Interceptors, on the other hand, are fixed or movable surfaces that offer a more streamlined approach to altering a boat's trim quickly, enhancing performance and responsiveness. Both systems are essential for optimizing ride quality and operational efficiency on the water.</p></p>
