<p><strong>The Remote Afterloading System Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>HDR Afterloaders</li><li>PDR Afterloaders</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The remote afterloading system market is divided into HDR (High Dose Rate) afterloaders and PDR (Pulsed Dose Rate) afterloaders. HDR afterloaders deliver a high dose of radiation in a short time, ideal for treating localized tumors, ensuring precise targeting while sparing surrounding healthy tissues. PDR afterloaders, in contrast, administer radiation in a series of pulses over an extended period, enabling more frequent tissue recovery. Both systems enhance patient safety, treatment efficacy, and the overall effectiveness of radiation therapies.</p></p>
