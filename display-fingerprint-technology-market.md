<p><p>Display fingerprint technology encompasses various methods of biometric identification integrated into screens. Optical identification uses light to capture fingerprint details through the display, providing efficient recognition through reflective patterns. In contrast, ultrasound identification employs high-frequency sound waves to penetrate the screen and capture intricate fingerprint features, offering enhanced accuracy and security. Both technologies facilitate seamless user authentication directly on devices, enhancing convenience while contributing to the growing biometric security market, particularly in smartphones and other smart devices.</p></p>
