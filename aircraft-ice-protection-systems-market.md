<p><strong>The Aircraft Ice Protection Systems Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Pneumatic De-icing Boots</li><li>Electro-thermal</li><li>Electro-mechanical</li><li>TKS Ice Protection</li><li>Bleed air</li><li>Passive</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Aircraft ice protection systems are crucial for ensuring flight safety in icy conditions. Pneumatic de-icing boots use inflatable rubber surfaces to break off accumulated ice. Electro-thermal systems apply heat directly to the aircraft's surface to prevent ice formation. Electro-mechanical systems utilize mechanical devices to remove ice effectively. TKS ice protection involves a fluid that is distributed over the airframe to prevent ice accumulation. Bleed air systems leverage hot air from the engine to keep surfaces clear of ice, while passive solutions rely on aerodynamic designs to minimize ice buildup.</p></p>
